        Hi, I'm Tina Chen (陈倩) — an investment professional with 7+ years across private equity, investment banking, and equity research.

        My career has spanned Warburg Pincus, Goldman Sachs, and Morgan Stanley, with a focus on Asia digital infrastructure and fintech. I've worked on deals ranging from a US$470M data center investment in China to pan-Asia platform scaling for a 1GW hyperscaler network.

        I'm drawn to the intersection of physical infrastructure and AI — the data centers, power grids, and capital structures that make intelligence at scale possible. I write to clarify my own thinking, and occasionally to share what I find.

        Outside of work, I paint in oils and am deeply interested in sociology (社会学) and anthropology (人类学). I studied Economics & Finance with a minor in Art History at the University of Hong Kong, and spent time at Wellesley College.
